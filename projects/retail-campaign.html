Retail Campaign Optimization

Project Overview

Optimized targeting for a major retail client by applying predictive regression analytics to 420,000+ customer records—driving a $22.1K/month uplift and boosting model explanatory power to an R² of 0.706.

The Challenge

Broad-based marketing campaigns were delivering diminishing returns, with sub-optimal response rates and inefficient budget allocation.

Solution Approach

Engineered 420K+ profiles with demographics, purchase history, and engagement. Built Linear, Ridge, and Lasso models using scikit-learn. Validated with A/B tests.

Results & Impact

  • 📈 $22.1K/month uplift in revenue
  • 🧠 R² = 0.706
  • 🎯 Response lift of 16.4%
