Project Overview

+

Optimized targeting for a major retail client by applying predictive regression analytics to 420,000+ customer records—driving a $22.1K/month uplift and boosting model explanatory power to an R² of 0.706.

+
+
+

The Challenge

+

Broad-based marketing campaigns were delivering diminishing returns, with sub-optimal response rates and inefficient budget allocation.

+
+
+

Solution Approach

+

Data Engineering: Curated and feature-engineered 420K+ customer profiles, integrating demographics, purchase histories, and engagement metrics. Modeling: Built and compared multiple regression algorithms (Linear, Ridge, Lasso) in Python’s scikit-learn, using cross-validation to ensure robustness. Validation: Conducted A/B tests on top-segment cohorts to measure lift and refine targeting criteria.

+
+
+

Results & Impact

+

Revenue Impact: Delivered an incremental $22.1K/month in campaign revenue. Response Lift: Improved campaign response rates by 16.4% over the prior baseline. Model Performance: Achieved an R² of up to 0.706, enabling stakeholders to allocate 18% more budget to high-value segments.
