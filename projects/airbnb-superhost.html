Airbnb Superhost Program Analysis

Project Overview

Used Lasso regression on 10K+ listings to quantify Superhost density impact on revenue for non-Superhosts.

The Challenge

Determine the optimal Superhost share that boosts neighborhood-level earnings without saturation.

Solution Approach

Standardized data, modeled in scikit-learn, segmented by tract and seasonality.

Results & Impact

  • 🔍 22.2% optimal density found
  • 💵 $264.26/month gain per 1% increase
