Airbnb Superhost Program Analysis

Lasso regression showed each 1% rise in Superhost share increases $264.26/month in revenue.

← Back to Projects

Project Overview

Applied Lasso regression on 10K+ listings to quantify the Superhost network effect on neighborhood revenue.

The Challenge

Determine the optimal Superhost density that maximizes non-Superhost earnings without oversaturating neighborhoods.

Solution Approach

  • Cleaned and standardized listing features in Python.
  • Ran Lasso in scikit-learn; segmented w.r.t. tract-level Superhost share and seasonal factors.

Results & Impact

Identified a 22.2% density threshold; each 1% increase in Superhost share delivered $264.26 more monthly revenue to non-Superhosts, peaking after a 2.1-month lag and driving an 18.1% Q2 uplift.

Project Details

Tools Used: Python, Lasso Regression, Data Visualization, scikit-learn

Key Impact: $264.26/month revenue increase per 1% Superhost share
