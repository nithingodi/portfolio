Market Expansion Study

Analyzed 1.2M+ foot-traffic records in Missouri; found Wright County with top demand-to-competitor ratio.

← Back to Projects

Project Overview

Analyzed 1.2M+ SafeGraph foot-traffic events across 114 Missouri counties to pinpoint optimal sites for limited-service restaurant growth.

The Challenge

Locate counties with high consumer demand and low competitor density to maximize ROI on new restaurant openings.

Solution Approach

  • Ran SQL queries in BigQuery to filter counties with >50% middle-income households.
  • Calculated visits-per-location and competitor count ratios.

Results & Impact

Ranked Wright County #1 with a 2.5:1 demand-to-competitor ratio, forecasting a 14% higher first-year ROI relative to state average.

Project Details

Tools Used: Google BigQuery, SQL, Geospatial Analysis

Key Impact: Identified optimal expansion location with 2.5:1 ratio
