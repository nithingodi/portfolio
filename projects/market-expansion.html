Market Expansion Study

Project Overview

Analyzed 1.2M+ SafeGraph foot-traffic events across 114 Missouri counties to pinpoint optimal sites for limited-service restaurants.

The Challenge

Locate counties with high consumer demand and low competitor density for new restaurant openings.

Solution Approach

Ran BigQuery/SQL queries to filter counties with >50% middle-income households; calculated visits-per-location vs. competitor ratios.

Results & Impact

  • Ranked Wright County #1 (2.5:1 demand-to-competitor ratio)
  • Projected +14% first-year ROI vs. state average
