OTG Pod Operations Simulation

Identified billing bottleneck; cut wait time by 94.8% with a third server.

← Back to Projects

Project Overview

Used newsvendor and queuing-theory models to optimize an “On-The-Go” service pod, cutting customer wait times by 94.8%.

The Challenge

High utilization (u = 93.6%) on two servers caused mean queue times of 7.43 min, degrading service experience.

Solution Approach

Forecasted demand (58.6 orders/hr, CVa = 0.739); calculated safety stock for consumables; applied M/M/m queue formulas to evaluate adding servers.

Results & Impact

Adding a third server reduced average wait to 0.39 min (94.8% reduction), while safety-stock policies cut stock-out risk by 78%.

Project Details

Tools Used: Queueing Theory, Operations Research, Simulation

Key Impact: 94.8% reduction in customer wait time
