OTG Pod Operations Simulation

Project Overview

Used newsvendor and queueing-theory models to optimize an “On-The-Go” service pod, cutting customer wait times by 94.8%.

The Challenge

High utilization (μ = 93.6%) on two servers led to mean wait times of 7.43 min.

Solution Approach

Forecasted demand (58.6 orders/hr, CVa = 0.739); calculated safety stock; applied M/M/m formulas to evaluate adding servers.

Results & Impact

  • Third server cut wait time from 7.43 min to 0.39 min (–94.8%)
  • Safety-stock policies reduced stock-out risk by 78%
