Bankruptcy Prediction Model

Project Overview

Benchmarked eight algorithms in SAS EM and deployed a five-node ensemble to minimize false negatives in corporate-failure forecasts.

The Challenge

False negatives carry outsized financial risk — model must err on flagging potential bankruptcies.

Solution Approach

Preprocessed financial ratios and categorical factors; tested Polynomial Logistic, forward/backward selection, Lasso, Decision Tree, Gradient Boosting, Neural Network; tuned via 10-fold CV.

Results & Impact

  • Ensemble achieved a 3.2% false-negative rate (–15–23% vs. standalone models)
  • Prioritized accuracy on failure detection to mitigate risk
